Hyper-Local AI-Based Indian Meal Plan Generator

A Student Research Project on Personalized, Healthy Indian Diets

About the Project

This project aims to revolutionize meal planning for Indian households by leveraging artificial intelligence and hyper-local data. Our system generates personalized, nutritious meal plans that respect regional tastes, dietary restrictions, and seasonal ingredient availability. The goal is to promote healthier eating habits while celebrating India's culinary diversity.

Problem Statement

Traditional meal planning apps often ignore local food preferences, ingredient accessibility, and cultural nuances. Users struggle to find meal plans that are both healthy and practical for their region, leading to poor adherence and nutritional gaps.

Innovation Highlights

  • Uses hyper-local ingredient databases for meal suggestions.
  • AI adapts plans to individual health goals and regional cuisines.
  • Seasonal and budget-friendly recommendations.
  • Considers dietary restrictions (vegan, diabetic, etc.).
  • Simple, user-friendly interface for all age groups.

Tech Stack

  • Python (AI Model & Data Processing)
  • Pandas & Scikit-learn (ML & Data Analysis)
  • HTML & CSS (Frontend)
  • Streamlit (Prototype UI)
  • Open-source Indian food datasets

Our Unique Features

  • Hyper-local ingredient mapping for every Indian region.
  • Personalized meal plans based on health, taste, and availability.
  • Nutrition scoring for each suggested meal.
  • Easy export and print options for meal plans.
  • Continuous learning from user feedback.
